How Can I Get Support?

No official support is being offered.
Some options are:

  • Use the CyberBoard mailing list.

  • Post a message in the CyberBoard topic of the discussion board on the ConsimWorld web site.

  • As a last resort post a message with "CyberBoard" in the title to the newsgroup: rec.games.board.

Other users and/or myself can provide help in any of these areas to the maximum benefit of everyone.
